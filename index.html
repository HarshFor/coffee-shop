Our Philosophy

More than just a cup of coffee

Founded in 2012 by two childhood friends, Bean & Brew started as a humble cart at the local farmers market. We believed that great coffee shouldn't be a luxury—it should be a daily ritual accessible to everyone.

Today, we roast our own beans in small batches every Tuesday and Thursday. We partner directly with farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices. Every cup tells a story of the soil, the farmer, and the roaster.
